Paint Name= White Head Horse
Artist Name= Martin Pescador
From= Andes
Price= $219.95
15.8"W x 27.6"H
Weight= 0.6 oz

Description=
Wind ruffling his mane and forelock, a proud white horse wears an ornate leather
 bridle. The alert animal swivels its ears to catch his master's voice. "I liked 
the beauty of this horse. Its image is replete with regal elegance," says Martín 
Pescador of his work. 

Titled "Caballo cabeza blanca" in Spanish.
