Artist Name= Emmanuel
From= Andes

15.0"W x 22.0"H

Description=
Achieving astonishing clarity with watercolor, Emmanuel depicts a lime and an
 onion. Strong green stalks sprout from its papery skin. The still life is 
inspired by Pablo Neruda's poem "Ode to the Onion." 

Titled "Milagro de vida" in Spanish.
