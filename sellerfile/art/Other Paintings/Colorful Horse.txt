Thundering hooves gallop across the scene as sinuous muscles take shape in 
multicolored ribbons. Ricardo Siccuro creates an almost surreal image, 
realistic yet imaginary. The proud steed prances across a shadowy background 
in a playful work of art. 

Titled "Cavalo colorido" in Portuguese.
