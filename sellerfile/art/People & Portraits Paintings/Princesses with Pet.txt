From= India

Description=
Dinesh Kumawat paints a scene of palatial serenity as two princesses 
sit on a florid rug, playing with their peacock pet. Kumawat expertly 
captures the princesses' regal poise as he paints with oil on canvas.
 His attention to detail is exquisite as he painstakingly depicts their
 jewelries and the mehendi henna tattoos on their hands. An exquisite 
composition that captures the mood of palace life in ancient India.
