Paint Name= Peace in Mother's Arms
Artist Name= Ida Ratna Ningrum
From= Bali and Java
Price= $535.46
27.6"W x 35.4"H
Weight= 0.9 lbs

Description=
A warm breeze ripples emerald leaves and allamanda blossoms in an 
ambience of cool jade green. Enjoying the garden, a mother blissfully 
embraces her small daughter. "Peace is a dream for each mother,
" says Balinese artist Ida Ratna Ningrum. "Her prayers and hopes 
always fill her time. And the peaceful times are remembered as being 
in mother's arms."
