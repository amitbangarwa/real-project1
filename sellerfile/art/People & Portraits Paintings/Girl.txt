Description=
Wayan Suarjiwatman works in expressionist style to depict a Balinese girl.
 Smiling, she gazes candidly at the artist just beyond the viewer's field 
of vision. Suarjiwatman's palette of quiet color conveys youth, hope and 
innocence in an insightful portrait. He names the painting Dara, "Girl" 
in the language of Bali.
