Paint Name= Looking for Freedom
Artist Name= Harman
From= Bali and Java
Price= $452.66
27.6"W x 35.4"H
Weight= 0.9 lbs

Description=
A beautiful woman inhabits an impassioned world of crimson,
 its color expressing the intensity of Harman's feelings. 
"I am restless and dreaming of freedom," the Javanese artist confides. 
"I depict a woman who has opened the door of the birdcage and set the 
lovely creature free. The door of the cage should be opened so freedom 
can occur."
