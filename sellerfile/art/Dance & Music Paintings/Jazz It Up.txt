From= West Africa

Description=
Joseph Kwaw Besoabewie captures the spirit of a musician who sways to a 
jazz melody. Abstract in style, the painting reveals a metallic effect as
 music takes over the vibrant canvas. The man is one with his golden horn
 and moves to inherent African rhythms.
