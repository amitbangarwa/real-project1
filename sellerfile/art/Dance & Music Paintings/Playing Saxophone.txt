Description=
Absorbed in his music, a young man plays the saxophone with impassioned mastery.
 His tunes are strong and inviting, its rhythms dictating the pace of the artist
's brushwork. Ghanaian artist Bright Dankyi Mensah works with acrylics, expertly
 capturing the melodious environment created by his subject, the saxophonist.
