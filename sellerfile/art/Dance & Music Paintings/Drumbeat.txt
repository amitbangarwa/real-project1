Paint Name= Drumbeat
Artist Name= Agyarko Kotoko
From= West Africa
Price= $879.95
42.9"W x 34.7"H
Weight= 1.4 lbs

Description=
Four women move in unison as they pound a beat on African drums. 
"Music and dance are part of everyday life," artist Agyarko Kotoko affirms.
 "One's mood can be expressed through dance. This piece is to express the joy 
of a good harvest."
