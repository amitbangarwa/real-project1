Description=
Traditions and customs inspire the work of Ghanaian artist Godwin Adjei Sowah as
 he depicts a group of women known as the "Green Dancers." They perform the 
"Kete Dance" at the Ashanti chiefs' durbar. Every movement is choreographed 
to carry a message to the chief, as well as to the audience.
