Description=
Parinya Nanjai evokes the presence of Lord Ganesha with reverence and admiration.
 Painting with sublime detail, Nanjai portrays the beloved deity within ancient
 Thai stylizations. According to the Hindu faith, Ganesha is the son of Shiva 
and Parvati – he is the Lord of success and destroyer of evils, obstacles,
 vanity, selfishness and pride.
