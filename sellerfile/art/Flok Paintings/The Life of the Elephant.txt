Paint Name= The Life of the Elephant
Artist Name= Parinya Nanjai
From= Thailand
Price= $177.95
22.0"H x 19.3"W x 1.8"D
Weight= 4.4 lbs

Description=
Wearing colorful silks, a rare white elephant is an imposing image.
 Gold rings adorn his tusks and his ankles are encircled with bracelets.
 Pale skin takes on a regal glow as Parinya Nanjai paints a beautiful image
 symbolic of power in Thailand. He presents it in a frame of teak and bamboo.
