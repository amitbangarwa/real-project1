Description=
Tumbling rhythmically to shore, gentle waves wash the sandy beach with 
sensations of peace, as the herons catch small fish. Ketut Sundra creates 
an enchanted vision of Bali, where mountain and hills can be seen in the 
distance, and palm trees reach toward a sky caressed with clouds.
