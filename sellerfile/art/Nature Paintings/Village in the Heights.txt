Description=
Nestled between jade and emerald fields, the town of Ahuaytia offers a 
safe haven at 12467 feet above sea level. "The view is spectacular!" 
says Alcides Medina of his source of inspiration. Painting with oils,
 he masterfully captures the calm and tranquility of Ahuaytia. 

Titled "Pueblo en las alturas" in Spanish.
