Description=
Light floods the senses, perceived through dazzling white clouds above a 
landscape of fiery crimson. To one side, palm trees bask in the lucid rays, 
their rustling fronds conveying a sensation of peace. Peru's Gabriel Juarez 
captures this magical moment in oils on canvas. 

Titled "Palmeras y cielo" in Spanish.
