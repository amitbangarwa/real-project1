Warmth radiates from the center of this 
dreamlike canvas by Leonidas Zavaleta. 
Enveloped in clouds is a small house high 
on a mountaintop. It seems to sway, gently 
rocked to the lullaby of the wind. 

Titled "Reflejo de una casa" in Spanish.
