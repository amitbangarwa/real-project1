Description=
Ganesha appears like a vision on a sunset sky, 
its warm colors reassuring of the deity's benevolent 
heart. As the mystical Remover of Obstacles and Lord 
of Knowledge, Ganesha is a much beloved deity from the 
Hindu pantheon. Painting with oils, Sunil Jaiswal depicts 
Ganesha holding a cup with his favorite dessert.
