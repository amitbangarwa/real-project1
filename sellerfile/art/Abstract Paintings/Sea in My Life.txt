Description=
Blue predominates in a canvas of luminous color. Caressed by clouds,
 patches of sunlight illuminate strokes of emerald and crimson.
 Andrea evokes a dream world of sheer sensation to illustrate a 
love poem by Dora Forletti named "Sea in My Life." 

Titled "Mar en mi vida" in Spanish.
