Artist Name= Bright Dankyi Mensah
From= West Africa
Price= $350.96
20.1"W x 25.2"H
Weight= 0.6lbs

Description=
Guarded by the masks of her ancestors, a woman appears like a mirage 
of beauty and tradition. Bright Dankyi Mensah paints her profile in 
a flurry of vibrant acrylic colors. He includes the millenary Duafe 
Adinkra symbol representing beauty and female qualities.
