Paint Name= Door with Adobe
Artist Name= Tere Turu
From= Mexico

24.0"H x 20.1"W x 0.3"D

Description=
Wide white lintels delineate the wooden door set in an adobe wall.
 Above and below, blue and white Talavera tiles adorn an older home 
in Mexico City. Bougainvillea tumbles across the entrance and Swedish
 ivy blooms in a pot. Working in watercolor, Tere Turu captures the
 sensations and beauty of a stately building. 

Titled "Puerta con adobe" in Spanish.
