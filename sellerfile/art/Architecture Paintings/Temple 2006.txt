Description=
Copper leaves mark the entrance to Pah Pao temple in Thailand,
 which is surrounded by lavish vegetation. A sense of serenity 
invades the senses, inspiring the work of Mon. He details the temple
 and the chedi next to it, as it is customary to find throughout 
Chiang Mai province. Mon paints with a palette of warm oil colors
 that evoke the tranquil mood that is offset by the setting sun. 

Arrives in rain tree wood frame.
