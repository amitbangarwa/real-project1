Artist Name= Alcides Medina Umeres
From= Andes

23.6"W x 31.5"H

Description=
Street lamps illuminate Arequipa as night falls and 
people continue with the busy rhythm of life. 
"I enjoy working with chiaroscuro techniques because 
of the contrasts they offer," explains Alcides Medina.
 Painting with oils, he masterfully details the architecture 
of the city, as well as the clothes, movement, and even attitude of the people. 

Titled "Arequipa nocturna" in Spanish.
