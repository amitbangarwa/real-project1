Description=
Florianopolis is a favorite theme for artist Luiza Kemp.
 This beautiful work depicts the bridge leading to the 
island in southern Brazil. She begins with original photographs
 cut in strips and arranged to create the sensation of movement.
 They form the basis for a composition in acrylics on canvas.
