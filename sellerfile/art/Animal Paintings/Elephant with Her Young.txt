Paint Name= Elephant with Her Young
Artist Name= Arruda Camara
From= Brazil
Price= $1079.95
39.4"W x 27.2"H

Description=
Arruda Camara demonstrates an absolute mastery of realism with this
 endearing animal portrait. It depicts a baby elephant hiding beneath 
his mother's massive stomach. The female reaches out to her young with 
a reassuring trunk. "This work represents maternal love," the Brazilian 
artist explains. 

Titled "Elefante com sua cria" in Portuguese.
