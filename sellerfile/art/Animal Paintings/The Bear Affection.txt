Paint Name= The Bear Affection
Artist Name= Andrea
From= Andes
Price= $1152.95
50.4"W x 51.2"H
Weight= 1.9lbs

Description=
Sunlight surrounds a bear in a golden halo as the great bruin roams 
through a mountain meadow. "Such a beautiful bear, affectionate, 
lazy and loving. That bear is soft, ticklish, and a trickster when he plays,
" Andrea muses. Bears are a favorite theme for this talented Andean artist. 

Titled "El oso cariño" in Spanish.
