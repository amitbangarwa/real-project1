Dawn arrives with a feast of colors marking the end of night school.
 A pupil is already asleep under the glaring stare of the owl teacher. 
A charismatic watercolor composition by Azucena Barrios who believes owls
 represent wisdom, knowledge and nocturnal mysteries. 

Titled "¿Dormido en tu primera lección?" in Spanish.
