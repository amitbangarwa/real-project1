From= Thailand

31.9"H x 39.4"W x 1.8"D

Description=
Framed by rustling leaves, banana blossoms give way to a stalk of golden fruit.
 Nitaya Tamwong captures a beautiful image of Thailand. During its cycle,
 the plant feeds insects, birds and eventually mammals. Thus the artist 
explores the beginning of elements to support all kinds of life.
