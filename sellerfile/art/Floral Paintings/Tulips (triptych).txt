For Mexican artist, Mariana González, muralist Diego Rivera had a 
strong influence on her work. Yet she demonstrates her own style 
with this colorful triptych. Brilliant tulips bloom realistically 
on emerald stems. 

Titled "Tulipanes" in Spanish.
