Artist Name= Sunil Jaiswal
From= India

22.0"W x 27.6"H
Weight= 0.7 lbs

Description=
Little Ganesha rests in the arms of his beloved mother, Parvati. 
The rotund deity with the head of an elephant lifts one hand in blessing;
 with the other, he toys with his mother's hair. "I have tried to convey 
the love of mother and child in this painting. Everyone is a child in his 
or her mother's eyes," artist Sunil Jaiswal confides.
