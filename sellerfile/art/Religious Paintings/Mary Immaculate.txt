Clouds part revealing the divine presence of Mary Immaculate in a painting
 by Rivero. "I draw inspiration from the art of the Renaissance as well as 
the Cuzco School of Religious Portraiture," explains the Peruvian artist. 
Working with oils, he paints a vivid portrait of the Madonna which he
 surrounds with gilt applications. 

Titled "Virgen imaculada" in Spanish.
