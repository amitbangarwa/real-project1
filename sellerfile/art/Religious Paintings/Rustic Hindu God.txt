Description=
Parinya Nanjai invokes the presence of Lord Ganesha with reverence and 
admiration. Painting with sublime detail, Nanjai portrays the beloved 
deity with ancient Thai stylizations. According to the Hindu faith, 
Ganesha is the son of Shiva and Parvati – he is the Lord of success 
and destroyer of evils, obstacles, vanity, selfishness and pride. 

Nanjai frames the painting in rustic bamboo.
