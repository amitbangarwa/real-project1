Description=
Kali ma takes the form of an angry goddess in this Madhubani painting 
by Devendra Kumar Jha. According to Hindu mysticism, Lord Shiva is the
 creator while his consort Kali is the destroyer. She dances on her prostrate
 husband while holding the heads of demons. The artist creates these powerful
 images using crushed wet rice and natural colors on handmade paper. 

As each painting is an individual creation, each is unique.
 Colors and motifs may vary slightly from the picture.
