Artist Name= Alberto Torres
From= Andes

20.1"H x 16.1"W x 1.2"D

Description=
Serenely beautiful, the Virgin Mary caresses her child with soft hands 
and cradles him in her lap. Working in the style of colonial Spanish art,
 Alberto Torres paints an exquisite portrait, detailing the halos and rich
 brocade in 24k gold leaf. The elegant painting is framed in finely carved
 cedar covered in gleaming bronze leaf. 

Titled "Virgen con el Niño Jesús" in Spanish.
